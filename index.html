ABEL

for the

Boston Design Center

The Boston Design Center has been described as an "eight story design mecca" by Boston Magazine. The BDC is the largest luxury design resource in New England.

The current navigation system relies on stationary maps and directories. Four maps are posted on each floor. One at each end of the central hallway, and in front of each elevator. However, you can only see the layout for the floor you are on.

The BDC's long hallways discourage tired shoppers. Showroom signs are hidden, and it is difficult to see into store fronts until you are standing right in front of them.

We envisioned a new way to get around the Boston Design Center. A smart companion to make your journey effective and efficient.
